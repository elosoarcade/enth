# Enth
The Enth is a Minecraft Bedrock add-on that brings a terrifying experience to your world. Based on Slenderman, this mob will give you more than just a scare.

The Enth is a mob that spawns at night in all biomes. It is completely immune to damage and knockback, and deals 99 damage per hit—so if you trigger its attack, survival is nearly impossible. However, this specter will only attack you if you look directly at its face (similar to the Enderman), meaning that if you don't look at it, you can run like your life depends on it.

This mob isn't particularly fast, making it perfectly possible to escape from it. And while it doesn't take damage from water, it also won't chase you into any body of water, so diving into a river is an excellent way to flee—especially if you've already made it angry.

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
